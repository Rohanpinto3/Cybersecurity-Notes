# Phishing

---

Phishing is a type of social engineering 

- **Phishing = Fake emails/texts that trick you into giving up sensitive info or installing malware.**

## **What It's Used For:**

### **1. Credential Theft**

- Fake login pages for banks, email, social media
- **Goal:** Steal usernames/passwords

### **2. Malware Delivery**

- Malicious attachments (PDF, Word docs)
- **Goal:** Install ransomware, keyloggers, backdoors

### **3. Financial Fraud**

- Fake invoices, CEO fraud ("Wire money now!")
- **Goal:** Direct money transfer to attacker

### **4. Data Collection**

- Fake forms asking for personal info
- **Goal:** Identity theft, espionage

---

## **Common Goals of Phishing:**

1. **Initial Access** → Get into company network
2. **Credential Harvesting** → Steal login information
3. **Financial Gain** → Direct theft or ransomware
4. **Espionage** → Steal data/intellectual property
5. **Spreading** → Use your account to phish others

---

# **Smishing vs Vishing** - Both are **phishing variants**:

---

## **Smishing = SMS Phishing 📱**

- **Channel:** Text messages
- **Examples:**
    - "Your package delivery failed - click here"
    - "Bank alert: suspicious activity on your account"
    - "You won a prize! Claim now"
- **Goal:** Get you to click malicious links in texts

---

## **Vishing = Voice Phishing 📞**

- **Channel:** Phone calls
- **Examples:**
    - "This is Microsoft Support - your computer has viruses"
    - "IRS calling - you owe taxes, pay now or get arrested"
    - "Grandma? I'm in jail and need bail money"
- **Goal:** Social engineering over the phone

---

# **Spear Phishing vs Whaling** - Both are **targeted phishing**:

---

## **Spear Phishing 🎯**

- **Target:** Specific individuals or departments
- **Research:** Uses your name, job role, company info
- **Example:**
    - "Hi Sarah, from Accounting? Need you to verify this invoice."
    - Uses info from LinkedIn, company website

## **Whaling 🐋**

- **Target:** **C-level executives** (CEO, CFO, CISO)
- **Research:** Extensive - knows their projects, travel plans
- **Example:**
    - "Hi CEO, urgent wire transfer needed for acquisition deal."
    - Pretends to be other executives or lawyers

---

## **Key Difference:**

- **Spear Phishing** → **Any employee** (more common)
- **Whaling** → **Big fish only** (higher value, less frequent)
