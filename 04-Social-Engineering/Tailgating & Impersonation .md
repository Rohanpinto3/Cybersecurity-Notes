# Tailgating & Impersonation

---

**Tailgating & Impersonation** - **Physical Social Engineering** 🚪🎭

---

## **Tailgating ("Piggybacking")**

- **What:** Following someone through a secure door without proper access
- **How it works:**
    - Wait near secure entrance
    - Act like you belong when employee badges in
    - "Can you hold the door? I forgot my badge"
- **Goal:** Gain physical access to restricted areas

---

## **Impersonation**

- **What:** Pretending to be someone you're not
- **Common Roles:**
    - **IT Support** - "Need to check your computer"
    - **Delivery Person** - "Package for accounting"
    - **Fire Inspector** - "Annual safety check"
    - **New Employee** - "First day, can you help me?"
- **Goal:** Gain trust + access to systems/information
