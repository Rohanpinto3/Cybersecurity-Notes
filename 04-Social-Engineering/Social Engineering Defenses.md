# **Social Engineering Defenses**

---

## **1. Employee Training & Awareness**

- **Phishing simulations** - Regular test emails
- **Recognition training** - Spot red flags
- **Reporting procedures** - "See something, say something"

## **2. Technical Controls**

- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** - Even if password stolen
- **Email filtering** - Block suspicious emails
- **Web filtering** - Block malicious sites
- **Least privilege** - Limit access to what's needed

## **3. Physical Security**

- **Badge access** - No tailgating
- **Visitor logs** - Track who enters
- **Clean desk policy** - No sensitive info left out

## **4. Verification Processes**

- **Call-back procedures** - Verify unusual requests
- **Two-person rule** - For sensitive operations
- **Digital signatures** - Verify email authenticity

## **5. Policies & Procedures**

- **Clear security policies** - Everyone knows the rules
- **Incident response plan** - What to do when attacked
- **Regular audits** - Find weaknesses before attackers do
